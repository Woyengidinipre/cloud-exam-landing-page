Our Mission

To create a dynamic, cloud-powered archive that empowers the Ijaw people by preserving oral traditions, historical accounts, cultural artifacts, and more, accessible anywhere, anytime.Our mission is to preserve and promote the rich cultural and historical heritage of the Ijaw people by creating a centralized, cloud-based platform. This platform will serve as a digital archive that brings together oral histories, traditional knowledge, artifacts, and important documents that have been scattered or inaccessible. Through this initiative, we aim to empower the Ijaw community and make our stories and legacy accessible to future generations, educators, researchers, and the global audience interested in African history and identity.
